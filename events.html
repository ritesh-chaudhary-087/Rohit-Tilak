Swast Diwali - Mast Diwali

Dr. Rohit Tilak, in collaboration with the Lokmanya Tilak Pratisthan and Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth, organized the "Swast Diwali, Mast Diwali" initiative. This event aimed to provide essential Diwali items, including festive snacks and other necessities, at affordable prices to the general public. The initiative took place from November 2 to November 5 at Natu Baug Maidan on Bajirao Road. The primary goal was to ensure that citizens could access quality products at reasonable rates, especially during times of economic hardship. The event featured around forty stalls offering various products, with an emphasis on maintaining quality while keeping prices low. Additionally, cultural programs and entertainment activities were organized to enhance the festive spirit.

Celebrate with Heart, Celebrate Smart Diwali is about connection, warmth, and giving. With Swast Diwali - Mast Diwali, you’re not only shopping wisely, but also supporting a community-driven movement focused on fair trade, local empowerment, and festive joy.
