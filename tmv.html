Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth

With a rich legacy of over 100 years, Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth is a premier university in Maharashtra. We have continuously evolved to meet the changing needs of education. We play a pivotal role in shaping young minds. TMV’s mission is to cultivate independent thinking, creativity, and practical intelligence. We strive to provide quality education at an affordable cost. Vidyapeeth equips students with the skills needed to face global challenges.

Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth offers a diverse range of UG, PG, and Ph.D. programs across seven faculties: Arts & Fine Arts, Ayurveda, Moral & Social Sciences, Modern Sciences & Professional Skills, Management, and Health Sciences. Beyond academics, we foster a vibrant campus life with an active Arts Circle, an NSS unit, and strong encouragement for sports activities. To support holistic student development and career growth, TMV has established key initiatives such as the International Students’ Cell, Placement Cell, Centre for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship Education, and Research & Development Centre etc. We have also set up the Centre for Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development, which aims to provide high-quality professional skills to individuals seeking to enhance their employability and entrepreneurial abilities.

Global Recognition

The curricular at Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth are inspired by Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak’s educational philosophy. It emphasizes the holistic development of students and the creation of empowered, responsible citizens who contribute to society and the nation. Guided by this vision, we strive for academic excellence. Our curriculum balances theoretical knowledge with practical application. We use interactive and experiential learning strategies. We actively encourage continuous assessment and constructive feedback to enhance Vidyapeeth’s growth and development. Our commitment to excellence and innovation ensures that we continue to adapt, evolve, and provide quality education that meets the needs of students and society.

Vision

To provide holistic education by integrating traditional wisdom with modern knowledge, ensuring that quality education is accessible to all, with a focus on empowering individuals and making education available to even the most marginalized communities.

Mission

To foster independent thinking, creativity, critical analysis, and practical intelligence among students.

To maintain a harmonious balance between high academic standards and affordability, ensuring education remains within reach for all.

To continuously equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in a rapidly evolving global environment.

About TMV

Deemed to be University"

In 1987, the Government of India granted Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth the status of a 'Deemed to be University,' recognizing its significant contributions to education and marking its national recognition. Over the past 32 years, the Vidyapeeth has made remarkable strides in expanding and diversifying its academic offerings.

At the time it was conferred "Deemed University" status, the Vidyapeeth had three primary faculties: Ayurveda, Sanskrit, and Social Sciences. Since then, the institution has grown to include new faculties in Modern Sciences, Management, Health Sciences, and Law. This expansion was made possible under the visionary leadership of Dr. Deepak Tilak, the current Chancellor, whose guidance has empowered the Vidyapeeth to adapt and thrive in a rapidly changing global environment.

Today, Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth offers a wide range of postgraduate programs such as M.Sc. in Computer Applications, MBA, MSW, LLB, MJ, M.Lib.I.Sc, M.A. in various disciplines (Social Sciences, Marathi, Hindi, English, Japanese, Sanskrit, and Indology), Ayurved, and M.Com. The Vidyapeeth also provides undergraduate programs in fields like BCA, BBA, Journalism, Hotel Management (BHMCT), Physiotherapy (BPT), Nursing (B.Sc.), B.Com, and B.A., offering a diverse academic experience to students across multiple disciplines.
