Dr. Rohit Deepak Tilak

The Custodian of Heritage,
the Architect of Tomorrow

Vice-President of TMV, and General Manager of daily Kesari Dr. Rohit Deepak Tilak, a distinguished scion of the magnanimous Tilak lineage, is the proud heir to the enduring legacy of Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak, the legendary freedom fighter and revered nationalist.

Born into the 5th generation of a family steeped in the values of patriotism, education, and social service, Dr. Rohit Tilak personifies the harmonious blend of tradition and modernity that his ancestors championed.

Healthcare

Dr. Rohit Tilak is committed to improving healthcare access for underprivileged communities. Through his association with Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth (TMV), he indirectly supports health education initiatives aimed at producing skilled healthcare professionals.

His efforts help strengthen the foundation of public health by promoting education in fields like nursing and healthcare services. His vision is to create a more inclusive healthcare system by fostering accessible health education for all.

Education

Dr. Rohit Tilak is dedicated to enhancing the educational landscape in Maharashtra. As a professor and executive council member at TMV, he focuses on improving academic standards and upgrading educational infrastructure.

He strives to ensure that underprivileged students have access to quality education through scholarships and inclusive learning environments. His goal is to empower the youth through knowledge, equipping them with the skills needed for a better future.
